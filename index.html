ABOUT US

A Kathmandu based Rock Band with Mandip Upreti on Vocal, Prashant Khadka on Lead Guitar, Lebi Bhabichanon drums and Aamos Bogati on Bass guitar, who like to play classic/hard rock. At the time, when Mandip was looking for someone to accompany him with guitar for his song met Prashant Khadka( Founder Member) in the studio. After couple of sessions, Prashant approached Mandip for the gig/shows and now the band perform in different clubs in K-Town. Soon the band is also coming up with their debut album Roadway to Destiny.

Thank You all for all the Love everyone of you have shown us. We have been working hard to bring good music to our fans/followers/wellwishers.
